Individualised Learner Records (ILR)

The Individualised Learner Record (ILR) is an on-going collection of data about learners and the learning undertaken by them from learning providers in the Further Education (FE) and Skills sector.

What is the Further Education (FE) and Skills sector?

The FE and Skills sector includes: Further Education Colleges, former External Institutions, Sixth Form Colleges, Training Organisations, Local Authorities, Academies, and Voluntary and Community Organisations.

You can use this service to:

  • Submit ILR data
  • View your ILR history

Earnings Adjustment Statement (EAS)

The Earnings Adjustment Statement (EAS) is an online process used to claim funding that is not reported in the Individualised Learner Record (ILR).

You can use this service to:

  • Manage your EAS return
